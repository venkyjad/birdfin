Saving

All your questions answered about Saving.

  All catagories

  • How do I open a Savings Bank Account for my Child, will Birdfin help?


    While you begin the registration process on BIRDFIN, you get an option to opt for any assistance in opening a Savings Bank Account with ICICI.

    If you select the checkbox, an ICICI Bank representative will get in touch with you and help you with the entire process of opening your child’s savings account with the bank.

  • Do you have a preferred banking partner to open my child’s savings bank account?


    Yes, it is our partner Bank, ICICI Bank Ltd.

  • What if I already have an existing savings bank account for our child?


    You can use the same account. However, it is advised to log all the BIRDFIN related transactions to that account on the BIRDFIN Digital Diary.

  • How can I transfer money into my child’s savings bank account on a regular basis?


    You can transfer the money like any other transaction you make online. On BIRDFIN you can set automatic or manual credit mode depending on your preference.

  • What are the intervals I can transfer money into my child’s account?


    You can do it on a monthly basis.

  • Is there a limit for transfer into children savings bank account?


    The value of 30% of the crystals earned, needs to be transferred to the child’s Savings Account every month.

  • How will I open a savings bank account for my child?


    BIRDFIN has partnered with one of the leading Banks in the country, ICICI Bank Ltd. for all transactional activities on BIRDFIN.

    While you begin the registration process on BIRDFIN, you get an option to opt for any assistance in opening a Savings Bank Account with ICICI.

    If you select the checkbox, an ICICI Bank representative will get in touch with you and help you with the entire process of opening your child’s Savings account with the Bank.

  • What is Pocket Wallet and how does it work?


    POCKET is VISA powered eWallet for your child provided by ICICI Bank. It can be used to send money online, recharging mobiles, shopping anywhere (online/offline), pay bills and much more. The crystals earned by your child would be converted to real money through this wallet only upon your approval.
