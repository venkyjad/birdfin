Birdfin helps children learn money, before they earn
money

The first ever money management and learning app that helps children understand money better through its “teach like a friend” approach. We know as parents, you think it would mean your child is glued up to their phones but guess what, it is completely the opposite! The app is driven by a smart access control system wherein parents can assign “tasks” to their kids and upon successful completion of the task, Birdfin rewards them in the form of “Crystals”, the world’s first digital currency for families which eventually gets converted into real currency!

Aimed at introducing and explaining to children the concept and importance of handling money effectively during their foundation years. All of it through a series of fun-filled activities to be carried out with their families!.


Help children learn the mantra of money

Apart from teaching children how to save money, we also help them understand the efforts which go into getting money flowing in their homes and how it is important to learn the money lessons. Youngsters get to learn many things through the activities assigned to them, varying from day to day activities to new and challenging ones too. Moreover, they also learn about the value systems, the morals, and ethics involved in the world of money, helping them to grow into adults who understand the mantra of money better.


Let children earn for a job well done

We believe in rewarding children for their honest efforts in a form which is both favourable and useful for them. Hence the concept of earning “crystals” through child-friendly money game and planner. Once the children to-do list is completed, the crystals in their digital wallets get converted to real currency linked to their bank accounts.


Enable children to save for their goals

Most often children fail to understand the hardships their parents go through in getting their favourite birthday present. The idea here is to educate them about financial security and inculcate the joy of having achieved something on their own by teaching them how to budget and save effectively for their goals. Interestingly, it is a challenge for many parents and we are here for it!


Empower children to spend wisely

We want to help your children identify their expenditures and spend their savings wisely, through a practical approach in their formative years only to make their future learning better. Parents can monitor their child’s activities and be assured of their financial upbringing wisely teaching them all about money like a friend.
