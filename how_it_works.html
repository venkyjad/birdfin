Hello ‘Crystals’ World’s first digital currency for families

Birdfin is for every member of the family. Parents can create a list of household activities for the children to do, who in turn get rewarded for completing them. Children can earn, manage, save and spend their crystals as real money with a dynamic and real-time pocket money manager and digital wallet.

The entire ecosystem is based on a strong footing of learning, which is conducted through various financial learning modules to teach children the basics of money, which is, earning, saving and spending. Also included in the application is a digital diary which helps children stay connected with their peers through social media. A chatroom induces communication, thereby, enhancing social skills and collaboration. Parents can also open and manage the spends of their children via a real banking account for minors.

With a host of e-commerce options in the Birdfin app, children can select how they want to spend their earnings and the parents can approve or reject the choices made.

This is how Birdfin works

For Parents

  • Easy task scheduling & tracking

    The app has a predefined inbuilt Tasks Library segment of different categories for different age-brackets. You can select one or more chores from multiple categories for different age-brackets from this repository of pre-defined tasks! You can select tasks of different difficulty levels for different age brackets as well! The app also allows you to create your own task, assign and schedule it for either or all of your children! Tasks can be recurring or non-recurring and can be auto-scheduled for the upcoming week.

    You have the choice to approve or deny the task completed as per your evaluation and satisfaction to help your child get better at it! You can very easily track the progress of your child’s behavioral growth and the speed of task completion through the task manager in the form of a digital diary.

  • Alerts & advice

    We assure you that we will take care of your child’s’ financial and behavioural growth as best as we can. Our integrated system helps you track your child’s activities on the app and see how they are handling money through the spending and saving tracker and pocket money manager. The moment you feel you need to assist your child further, you get an alert helping you to sit down with your child to give them a few financial tips.

  • Simple categorisation

    We know you love customising, well you can do it on Birdfin too! Parents can choose any age bracket and add tasks according to the difficulty level they find appropriate for each of their children! After all the game has to be fair, trust is important between a child and a parent. We encourage parents to gift Birdfin to their children depending on the way they want it for kids ages 5-8 years, tweens ages 9-13 years and teens ages 14-18 years.

For Children

  • Learning videos & lessons

    The best way to learn something is by doing it. And if your children want to learn how to complete a particular task, we have it ready for them. Your children get access to our resource library in order to learn better and quicker. This surely is going to be an insightful experience for your children!

  • Saving & spending

    Children get to understand what is a viable purchase, a reasonable demand, and a useful expenditure and how to save for them effectively. They also understand how important and fulfilling it is to donate and do something for the lesser privileged ones in the world and inculcate social skills. You can closely observe and analyze the saving and spending patterns of your children through the app. The idea is to help you in understanding their material behaviour and moral value growth better and assist them whenever they are moving off the track.

  • Safe & secure

    Birdfin is a safe secure app for children. With the smart access control mechanism, as parents, you have the ultimate authority to monitor every activity of your children and modify your decisions based on your children’s progress. Also, we make sure that the content presented to your child is age-friendly and not harmful in any way. Transaction details are safe at any point of the time and no data is shared with any third party at all.
